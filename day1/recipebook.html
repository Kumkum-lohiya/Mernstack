Delicious Chocolate Cake

Nutritional Information
Calories Per Serving
320 8g

    Ingredients
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder

    Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree fahrenheight
  2. Mix the flour,sugar,and butter.
  3. Add the eggs and cocoa powder.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
